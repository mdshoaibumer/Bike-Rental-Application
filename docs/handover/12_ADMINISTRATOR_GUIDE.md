# Administrator Guide (Business Operations)

Welcome to the Bike Rental Admin Dashboard. This guide will help you manage your fleet and customers.

## 1. Dashboard Overview
Upon logging in with your Admin credentials, you will see real-time metrics:
*   **Today's Revenue**: Total money collected from completed and active bookings today.
*   **Pending Approvals**: Customers waiting for you to accept their booking requests.
*   **Active Rentals**: Bikes currently out on the road.

## 2. Managing the Fleet (Bikes)
*   Navigate to the **Bikes** tab to see your entire inventory.
*   **Add a Bike**: Tap the `+` icon. Enter the Registration Number, Brand, Model, and Price per Day.
*   **Maintenance**: Tap an existing bike and change its status to `MAINTENANCE` to prevent customers from booking it.

## 3. Processing Bookings
This is your daily workflow. Navigate to the **Bookings** tab:
1.  **Approve**: Tap a `PENDING_APPROVAL` booking. If the bike is physically available and in good condition, tap **Approve**.
2.  **Mark Ready**: Once washed and fueled, tap **Mark Ready for Pickup**.
3.  **Start Rental**: When the customer arrives and shows their ID, hand over the keys and tap **Start Rental**. The status becomes `ACTIVE`.
4.  **Complete Rental**: When the customer returns the bike, inspect it for damages, then tap **Complete Rental**. The security deposit can now be refunded.

## 4. Customer Directory
*   Navigate to the **Customers** tab to view all registered users.
*   You can view their Driving License number and booking history to verify returning customers.
