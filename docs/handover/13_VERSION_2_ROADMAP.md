# Version 2 Roadmap & Future Enhancements

While Version 1.0 (MVP) delivers a complete booking and operational pipeline, the following features are strongly recommended for Version 2.0 to scale enterprise operations.

## 1. Fleet & Operations
*   **GPS Tracking**: Real-time IoT integration for tracking active rentals.
*   **Multi-Branch Support**: Enable customers to pick up from Branch A and drop off at Branch B.
*   **AI Maintenance Prediction**: Analyze mileage and age to proactively flag bikes for service.
*   **Vendor Management**: Allow third-party bike owners to list their vehicles on the platform (Marketplace model).

## 2. Customer Experience
*   **Digital KYC Validation**: Automated API integration with Indian Government databases for instant Driving License verification.
*   **Coupon & Loyalty Engine**: Implement discount codes and points-based rewards.
*   **Corporate & Subscription Plans**: Long-term leasing options for enterprise clients (e.g., delivery fleets).

## 3. Financial & Legal
*   **Automated Invoice Generation**: PDF generation matching GST formatting rules.
*   **Dynamic / AI Pricing**: Surge pricing during weekends or high-demand festivals (e.g., Diwali).

## 4. Platform Reach
*   **Regional Languages**: Localization for Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, etc.
*   **iOS Application**: Replicate the Android UX in Swift/SwiftUI.
