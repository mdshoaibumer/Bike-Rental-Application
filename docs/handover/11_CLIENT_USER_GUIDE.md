# Client User Guide (Customer)

Welcome to the Bike Rental Application! This guide will help you book your first ride.

## 1. Getting Started
*   **Login**: Open the app and enter your 10-digit mobile number. You will receive an OTP via SMS. Enter it to log in.
*   **Profile Setup**: Before booking, navigate to your Profile to upload your Driving License details.

## 2. Finding a Bike
*   Use the **Search Bar** to find specific models (e.g., "Royal Enfield Classic 350").
*   Use the **Filter Menu** to filter by categories like *Scooters*, *Cruisers*, or *Sports*.

## 3. Booking a Ride
1.  Tap on your desired bike.
2.  Select your **Pickup Date & Time** and **Return Date & Time**.
3.  The system will calculate your total rental amount and security deposit.
4.  Tap **Confirm Booking**.

## 4. Picking Up Your Bike
1.  Once confirmed, your booking status will show as `PENDING_APPROVAL`.
2.  The store admin will verify the bike and change the status to `READY_FOR_PICKUP`.
3.  Visit the store. The admin will hand over the keys and change your status to `ACTIVE`. Enjoy your ride!
